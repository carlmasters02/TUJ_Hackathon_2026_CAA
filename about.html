About CampusBites

Here at CampusBites, we are dedicated to helping students discover and enjoy the best food options on their campus. We understand that finding delicious and convenient meals can be a challenge for busy students, which is why we created a platform that connects them with local food vendors and provides real-time updates on menu offerings and promotions.

We accomplish this by partnering with a wide range of food vendors on campus, from popular chains to local favorites. Our platform allows students to easily browse menus, place orders, and receive notifications about special deals and discounts. We are committed to providing a seamless and enjoyable food ordering experience for students, while also supporting local businesses on campus.

CampusBites story

CampusBites was founded by students who were passionate about food and wanted to make it easier for their peers to access delicious meals on campus. We started as a small project during a hackathon, and quickly realized the potential to create a platform that could benefit students and local food vendors alike. Since then, we have been working tirelessly to develop and improve our platform, and we are proud to say that we have helped thousands of students discover new food options and support local businesses on their campus.
