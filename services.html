CampusBites Schedule/Services

CampusBites proudly features three favorite food trucks that have been catering to Temple University Japan students, faculty, and campus events. From quick between-class bites to full lunch service, our partner trucks bring reliable schedules, fresh ingredients, and student-friendly prices right to TUJ.

Sakura Street Grill

Sakura Street Grill is known across Temple University Japan for hot rice bowls, teriyaki wraps, and comfort food made fast. Their team regularly caters student organization events and weekday lunch rushes with quick service and consistent quality.

Tokyo Taco Truck

Tokyo Taco Truck brings a Japanese-Mexican fusion menu to TUJ, including loaded tacos, spicy chicken burritos, and vegetarian options that sell out fast. They are a top pick for evening programs and club socials.

Noodle Lane Express

Noodle Lane Express serves fresh ramen cups, udon bowls, and seasonal specials tailored for busy TUJ schedules. Their catering setup is ideal for open campus days, guest lectures, and department gatherings.

Weekly TUJ Catering Support

Together, these three food trucks help CampusBites deliver dependable weekly catering for Temple University Japan, with flexible menus for dietary preferences and event sizes. Book through CampusBites to bring trusted campus favorites to your next TUJ event.

  • Sakura Street Grill: Rice bowls, wraps, and crowd-friendly combos.
  • Tokyo Taco Truck: Fusion tacos, burritos, and late-day catering.
  • Noodle Lane Express: Ramen, udon, and seasonal noodle specials.
