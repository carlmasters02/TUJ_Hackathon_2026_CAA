CampusBites Profile

CampusBites is a student-focused food truck discovery and catering platform built to support fast campus life in Tokyo. We connect the TUJ community and nearby international universities with trusted food truck partners that deliver quality, consistency, and variety.

What We Focus On

  • Reliable weekly truck scheduling for class-hour convenience.
  • Affordable meal options designed for student budgets.
  • Easy event catering coordination for clubs and departments.
  • Strong partnerships with local vendors and campus organizations.

Mission

Our mission is to make campus dining easier to discover, easier to enjoy, and more community-driven. By combining local food culture with campus convenience, CampusBites helps students spend less time searching and more time enjoying great meals.
